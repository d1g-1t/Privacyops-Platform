## 1. General Provisions

This Privacy Policy has been developed in accordance with the requirements of Federal Law No. 152-FZ of July 27, 2006 "On Personal Data" and regulates the procedure for processing users' personal data by the Operator.

## 2. Categories of Personal Data Processed

| Category | List | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| Identification | Full name, email, phone | Registration and authorization | Consent (Art. 9 of 152-FZ) |
| Technical | IP address, cookies, user-agent | Security and analytics | Legitimate interest (Art. 6 Part 1 Clause 5) |
| Contractual | Payment details | Contract performance | Contract (Art. 6 Part 1 Clause 5) |

## 3. Purposes of Personal Data Processing

- Conclusion and performance of contracts with users
- Identification of users when contacting support
- Improving service quality and user experience
- Compliance with the requirements of the legislation of the Russian Federation

## 4. Personal Data Localization

In accordance with the requirements of Article 18(5) of Federal Law No. 152-FZ, the primary processing of personal data of Russian citizens is carried out on servers physically located on the territory of the Russian Federation (Sibir Data Center, Novosibirsk).

## 5. Retention Period

Personal data is stored for the duration of the contract plus 3 years, unless a different period is established by law.

## 6. Disclosure to Third Parties

The Operator may transfer personal data to third parties exclusively:
- With the explicit consent of the data subject
- Upon request of a court or authorized state bodies
- For contract performance (payment service providers, delivery services)

All third-party processors are required to enter into a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).

## 7. Rights of Personal Data Subjects

- Right to access their personal data (Art. 14)
- Right to rectify inaccurate data (Art. 21)
- Right to erasure of data (Art. 21)
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to withdraw consent

To exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@example.ru  
Response time: 30 days from the date of request.

## 8. Personal Data Security

The Operator applies the following organizational and technical measures:
- Data encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Data encryption at rest
- Role-based access control
- Audit logging of all personal data operations
- Regular security testing and risk assessment

## 9. Policy Changes

The Operator reserves the right to make changes to this Policy. In case of material changes, users will be notified by email at least 30 days before the changes take effect.
